Inspired from a terminated project, "TheCBL" for Combat Arms, LoLCBL aims to bring back similar functions to the original Tribunal system by Riot Games. In the case of LoLCBL however, users are able to submit reports for players who they believe are infringing upon the Summoner's Code - LoLCBL Staff would then reject or approve them according to a thorough, trusted and transparent process. If a player is found to be guilty their profile would then on be permanently flagged and all offenses, with provided evidence, publicly listed on their profile. To better understand our guidelines, please visit our Guidelines & FAQ section.
